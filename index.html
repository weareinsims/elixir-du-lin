Our Philosophy

Laundry was never
just laundry.

Élixir Du Lin was built on a simple belief: the clothes you wear every day deserve the same attention as the perfume you choose. We took the architecture of the world's most revered fragrances and translated them, note by note, into a format no one had touched.

Each box contains 18 sheets, each one releasing a carefully balanced composition as it works. Woody aromatics. Fresh marines. Deep orientals. Soft florals. The scent stays true through every wear.
